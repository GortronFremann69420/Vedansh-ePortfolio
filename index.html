Launcher Project

Sketched up MULTIPLE designs for a catapult/trebuchet that would launch a 100 gram projectile across a room, the one we landed with was one that used elactics to stress a beam that pivots on a pyramid base. Afterwards me and my group of three calculated the arc height and distance travelled by the projectile. On average the projectile travelled 18 feet.

Photoshop Glitch

3D Print Rocket Fins

Modeled some rocket fins in Blender, then fixed wholes in meshmixer and then I sliced (prepared the model for print, kind of like reviewing a PDF before 2D printing it) it in OrcaSlicer before printing and attaching it to a bottle rocket that we launched.

Nerdy Dungeon Thing

Modeled many fantasy items and placed them in a dungeon environment, basically a test/inspiration for my 3D ambience scene.
